The New Agent Console

Start the tour to know how the new agent console lets you better manage your leads and your listings.

Dashboard

What we offer

  • Bird's eye view of all that is happening in your account
  • Overview of your promoted projects, sale listings, and rent listings
  • Your credit consumption summary for the last 15 days
  • Filters you have set and the leads you have received against them

Profile

What we offer

  • A profile page to create your unique identity on CommonFloor.com
  • In addition to your name, address, and contact numbers, it contains details about your business and the areas you service
  • Your profile is visible to all property seekers on CommonFloor

What you can do

  • Fill details for your profile. Always keep your profile updated

Property Listings

What we offer

  • Unlimited listings for free
  • We deduct credits only on leads delivered to you
  • Manage your credits by setting a lead cap on each listing

What you can do

  • List your property. Fill all the details to get more responses
  • Modify lead cap. Default is 5 leads per listing
  • Pause listing if you no longer want to receive leads for it

Leads

What we offer

  • Get leads matching your filter criteria
  • We deduct credits only when you view contact details of a lead

What you can do

  • Set your filter criteria
  • View contact details of leads

Reports

What we offer

  • Detailed reports about the leads you consumed
  • Credit refund if a lead turns out to be a broker

What you can do

  • View and download reports
  • Request and track credit refunds. Refund request must be raised within 4 days of the lead being delivered
  • Contact your CommonFloor Sales Account Manager to purchase more credits

Team

What we offer

  • Manage your business better by adding team members to your CommonFloor account
  • Individual credit cap for each team member
  • Subscription filters for each team member

What you can do

  • Create team members
  • Allocate credits to each team member
  • Contact Agent Care to set up subscription filters for your team members

Have a Specific Question? Refer the FAQ Section
My Account
   My account is created by Sales Manager. How do I add more details in my profile?
Log on to your dashboard and navigate to My Account > My Profile from the left hand navigation bar. Here, you can add details about your business, areas where you operate etc.
   How do I check what type of leads am I subscribed for?
To check your current subscription of lead type, navigate to My Account > My Subscriptions. Here, you can review the lead filters (criteria for receiving leads) which are set for you. You can also set the limit on total credit consumption and limit on daily leads for these filters.
   I have a team of executives who works for me. Can I create their user accounts, assign them credits, and manage their accounts?
Yes, My team feature provides you the ability to create sub accounts, assign them credits for each type of leads. You can also view the credit consumption details of your team mates.
   What happens when I delete my team member’s account with listings and credits in it?
All listings and remaining credits will be migrated to the Parent (Main) account.
My Listings
   What happens to my existing premium and sponsored listing with your new transformed model?
All active Premium and Sponsored Listings will be converted to Basic Listings. For every premium listing credit, you will be able to post 4 Basic Listings. Similarly, for all sponsored listing credits, you will be able to post 8 Basic Listings. For these listings, you will continue to receive unlimited responses till they expire.
   I have just subscribed to CommonFloor. Can I get the previous unlimited response plan?
No, all new subscriptions will be migrated to the new lead based model. You will not be able to get the older subscription.
   What is the validity of an active listing in new system?
The listing validity remains the same. Rent listings are valid for 60 days and sale listings are valid for 120 days.
   I do not see the option to refresh a listing. Why?
Refresh option is not given to ensure that the listing with the most relevant content appears on top.
   I do not see the option to delete a listing. Why?
With refresh option removed, the normal course for the clients to make their listing appear on top of the search results would be to delete the listing and repost again. This is why we have removed the option to delete a listing.
   How do I ensure that my listing comes on top and remains on top of the search results?
CommonFloor shares listing guidelines with clients on how to improve the listing score. Though the guidelines help you in getting better ranking of your listing, we cannot guarantee that it would stay on top for long time. Our sorting algorithm is mostly relevance based which is tuned to promote listings which the system understands to be in demand by the seekers.
   How do I ensure that my listing comes on top of the other listing from competitor?
We suggest to post as much relevant information about the property as possible. In case, the details posted by both agents is same, the system will take the reputation of the agent into consideration and will show the listing.
   What are the listing guidelines?
Click here to view the listing guidelines on how to improve the listing score. Though the guidelines help you in getting better ranking of your listing, we cannot guarantee that it would stay on top for long time.
   What do you mean by “Pause Listing”?
It means that the clients specifically express that they don’t want leads for certain period of time.
Reasons could be:
Off Season - He is going on a vacation or probably the client wants to promote other projects/listings and don’t want to spend money on leads generated from this listing/project.
Listing closed - It may also be possible that the client has already got responses and has finally converted (sold or rented) the listing.
Leads
   Where will I get leads from? How do you ensure I will get leads when you are cutting down on ads?
We are ensuring that the content of Listings/Projects/Localities is enriched with great details. Additionally, our ongoing efforts on SEO and SEM would help us attract more traffic. Our new user experience should help property seekers find properties they are looking for, more easily than ever before. This should significantly boost Lead Quantity.
   What do you mean by “Get Leads”?
This is same as pulling the lead from agent console in the previous version. You can filter specific requirement and pull the contact info by paying the credits shown against it.
   Do I get the same lead which is shared with other clients?
Project leads are exclusive and are not shared with anyone except the promoter. Locality leads will be shared with others.
   How do I report a bad lead? What is the criterion to report a lead?
You can use “Request Refund” option against a lead which is from an agent. We do not allow to report bad lead (bogus or insincere) as we do a quality check on each lead before sharing it.
You can request for refund within 4 days of lead being shared, post which the option will not be available.
   What is the turnaround time on refunding the credits?
We take a maximum of 3 working days to verify the lead and refund the credits.
   What happens when I request for refund on a lead?
Our Service Delivery team will verify if the lead reported by you is genuine or not. If it turns out to be an agent lead, they will process the refund else decline it.
Reports
   What do you mean by lead cap and credit cap?
Lead cap on a filter is maximum number of the leads you will receive in a day. Credit cap on a filter is the total number of credits you are willing to pay for leads of the particular filter.
   How do I see the lead report of my team member?
Navigate to Reports > Lead Report. Under Account, select appropriate team member name. The page will reload with the lead report of your team member.
